Relaxation and fun at Holiday Club Saimaan Rauha
For some, the best holidays are spent relaxing in the pool. For others, it's engaging in active hobbies and trying out different sports, while the rest enjoys the historical area and lakeside views. At Holiday Club Saimaan Rauha near Imatra, everyone can enjoy their holiday in their own way.

One of the largest indoor water parks in Finland
4 high-quality and diverse restaurants
Over 10 different activities for the whole family
Stay in a hotel room or holiday apartment
Host events, meetings and celebrations for 6–1800 people

Saimaan Rauha water park and day spa
At one of Finland's largest indoor water parks, you can enjoy a fun day with the whole family. The water park's pools, water slides, and water show ensure a day full of excitement. Balance it out with the warmth of the sauna world and a pampering session with Harmony Spa treatments.

Stay at Saimaan Rauha
Book your accommodation at the historical Castle hotel or stay in one of the Pavilion suites. For those needing more space, there are holiday apartments equipped with a grill and yard terrace.

Arrival
Holiday Club Saimaan Rauha is surprisingly near!

Address: Rauhanrinne 1, 55320 Rauha

From Imatra city center 6 km / 8 min by car
From Lappeenranta city center 35 km / 25 min by car
From Helsinki 250 km / 3 hours by car
In addition to arriving by car, Saimaan Rauha is also easy to reach by train (Imatra train station). For example, the train journey from Helsinki to Imatra takes just over 2,5 hours. Check the route on VR's website

Parking

There are plenty of free parking spaces in the area. Charging stations for electric cars are located by the main entrance.
