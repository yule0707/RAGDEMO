Thoughtfully made, effortlessly enjoyed
We want your holiday to feel effortless – while doing good behind the scenes. That means experiences that save natural resources, support local life, and let you make smart choices without extra hassle. In short: relax and enjoy, worry-free.

Responsibility is about caring
We design the processes, create smart solutions, and make sure everything is ready for your holiday. Then we invite you to join in with small, meaningful actions.

Take only the food you’ll eat, use energy wisely, or choose a local route on foot or by bike – and you’re part of something bigger.

For us, responsibility means caring: for people, for nature, and for the future. Together, we make every choice count.

Getting there, the responsible way
Our resorts are easy to reach by public transport. For example, Turun Caribia and Tampereen Kehräämö have great connections from the Helsinki area.

On each destination page, you’ll find tips for arriving by bus or train. And for example in Vierumäki, it is easy to explore the area on foot or by bike.

Smart choices on your plate
We serve plenty of delicious food at our resorts – but we encourage guests to take just the right amount at a time to help cut down on food waste.

Our restaurants feature local specialties, organic products, and tasty vegetarian options.

Move your snacks
Step outside and explore! Our resorts are surrounded by beautiful nature and plenty of trails – perfect for walking or cycling. Along the way, you’ll find rest spots and campfire areas, and you can easily grab snacks from our resorts before heading out.

Need a bike? Many of our destinations and holiday apartments have them ready for you to use.

And remember: leave nothing behind but your worries, so our shared environment stays clean and green!

Little actions, big impact
Responsibility isn’t hard—it’s built on small gestures and everyday actions. We make sure our team knows how to act responsibly in every situation.

When we switch off the lights, use water wisely, and recycle properly, we’re caring not just for the environment but for each other. Responsibility creates a workplace where people feel good about coming to work.

Because nothing should go to waste
We design our operations so that waste is sorted and recycled as efficiently as possible.

We are also creating sustainable solutions that generate as little waste as we can.

Don’t waste clean water
We do our best to make sure water is used only when needed, and we encourage our guests to do the same, whether in the water park, hotel rooms, or holiday apartments.

Sheets and towels aren’t washed mid-stay unless requested, so we save water where it counts.

The shared joy of ownership
When you own a holiday week, you share the destination with like-minded travelers. This reduces the impact of building and maintenance, because the same space serves many guests throughout the year. Shared ownership makes holidays efficient in the best way: you enjoy your own time without unnecessary consumption.

Shared use means spaces and services stay active year-round. That saves energy and materials, while every guest benefits from a ready, well-maintained environment. It’s responsible holidaying at its finest.

Water park warmth, straight to your room
The Vierumäki spa was designed with sustainability at its core. Heat energy from pools, shower wastewater, and exhaust air is recycled back into the building’s heating system, and pool pumps run only when needed, all helping to reduce energy consumption.

No disposable seat covers or paper towels here, and drink stations work without cups. Even the soap is eco-labeled – every detail supports an environmentally friendly holiday.

From the hot tub to the toilet
Clean water is a Finnish treasure. But it’s not needed everywhere—for example when flushing toilets.

At Katinkulta, we reuse water park water for hotel toilets. That way, we save fresh drinking water, and the nature says thank you.

In all our hotel spas, heat energy from exhaust air is recycled back into the building’s heating system.
