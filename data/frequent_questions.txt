Frequently asked questions
Below, you will find answers to frequently asked questions regarding holiday bookings, changes and gift cards.

Holiday reservations
Booking, changes, and payment methods
How do I make a new holiday booking?
The easiest way to make a new reservation is through our online store.

Select your desired destination
Choose the dates you wish to stay
Add the correct number of guests
Enter any promotional code if applicable
Choose the best accommodation for your holiday
Enhance your stay with additional services
Fill in your details, review the terms and conditions, and pay immediately or at the destination, depending on the reservation.
You will receive a confirmation of your reservation via email.

If you are unable to make a reservation through the online store, please contact our customer service.

Can I change or cancel my booked holiday?
The possibility of changes or cancellations depends on the destination, payment method, or any applicable promotions.

Hotel rooms: A reservation that is paid at the destination can be canceled free of charge up to 18:00 on the day of arrival.

Cancellations can be made via the link found in the booking confirmation for online store reservations, or otherwise by calling or using the cancellation form.

Prepaid reservations cannot be canceled or transferred.

Holiday apartments: A reservation can be canceled free of charge up to 14 days before the start of the booking using the form or by contacting our customer service.

The advance payment confirms the reservation, and the room is automatically held for a late arrival. The booking date can be changed once by calling customer service no later than 48 hours before the reservation begins. The reservation cannot be canceled. If the customer does not show up, the advance payment will not be refunded.

We recommend travel insurance to cover any unexpected situations.

There may be promotional exceptions to the change and cancellation conditions.

Read more about cancellations and booking terms and conditions

 

What payment methods are available in the online store?
You can pay in the online store with a debit or credit card (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), Mobile Pay, Klarna, or an online bank payment.

You can pay for hotel room reservations either online or at the hotel reception upon arrival. Holiday apartments are always paid at the time of booking.

With a gift card, you can make a booking in the online store, if your gift card includes a campaign code and you are booking a hotel room. Holiday apartments cannot be booked through the online store with a gift card.

If you have a value gift card without a campaign code, and you are booking a hotel room, use the campaign code LAHJAKORTTI when booking. When booking with a value gift card, the total amount of your reservation will be displayed, and you can pay it entirely or partially with the gift card.

The gift card will only be charged upon check-in. Please note that the gift card must be valid at the start of your accommodation.

Some campaigns may have restrictions that affect the available payment methods.

Why might my holiday include an apartment change?
An apartment change may occur during your holiday if your original apartment is not available for the entire duration of your stay. In that situation, we will move you to another similar holiday apartment. 

We aim to avoid changes whenever possible. However, if a change is necessary, we will inform you before your holiday begins. Our goal is to make any transition as smooth and stress-free as possible, so your holiday experience remains uninterrupted.

Is it possible to arrange an early check-in?
This depends on availability and the location. You can inquire about it at the hotel reception.

The standard check-in time is shown on your booking confirmation. You can also find check-in times and reception contact details on the location pages.

Can I use Epassi or Smartum vouchers as a payment method?
Yes, you can pay for the water park, day spa and activities using Epassi and Smartum exercise and culture vouchers at the reception and customer service points of the locations. Epassi and Smartum cannot be used for payments in the online store. Epassi and Smartum cannot be used as payment methods for gift cards.

My reservation
Is breakfast included in the price?
Breakfast is included in the price of the hotel rooms.

Guests staying in holiday apartments located at resorts can purchase a hotel breakfast for an additional fee. Breakfast is not available at our other holiday apartment locations.

You can purchase breakfast for the best price either during the booking process for your entire stay or at the resort. 

 

Is the use of the water park or pool area included in the price of the holiday?
You can purchase access to the water park/pool area for your entire stay during the booking process for an additional fee for the days you prefer. The accommodation price does not include access to the water park or pool area.

Our locations with a water park or swimming pool area are Katinkulta, Saimaan Rauha, Turku's Caribia, Tampere's Kehräämö, Kuusamo's Tropiikki, Saariselkä, and Salla. A water park and sauna world is still being planned for Vierumäki.

Please note that if you book a spa holiday through another booking site (e.g., Booking.com), the inclusion of the water park/pool area in the booking price may vary.

 

Is parking free?
Parking is subject to a fee at some of our locations. For more details, please see the location pages.

Is it possible to charge an electric car in the parking area?
Some of our locations have electric vehicle charging points. For more details, please see the location pages or inquire directly at the location about charging options.

Blankets and pillows at Holiday Club
The pillows and duvets we use are primarily microfiber-filled, fire-safe products designed for hotel use. Microfiber is hypoallergenic and maintains cleanliness well. 
The pillows and duvets in the holiday apartments are the same kind as the ones in the hotels and the Villas. 
Allergy rooms and allergy cleaning
We do not have separate allergy rooms at our locations, nor can we offer special allergy cleaning services. Only pet-free rooms are available. 

Allergies and special diets
Breakfast

The breakfast offers a variety of options for lactose-free diets.
The selection also includes gluten-free and vegan options. There is a dedicated gluten-free section in all of our breakfast restaurants, with its own margarine, bread, and sweet products. There's also warmed up gluten-free options.
For those following a vegan diet, our hotels always offer plant-based milk drinks, yogurt, and plant-based deli meats and cheese-like products.
Other meals

Restaurants always offer vegan, lactose-free, dairy-free, and gluten-free meals.
In addition, the restaurants are able to accommodate almost all diets and allergies quite well.
Are you allowed to bring your own hot tub/bath tub?
Unfortunately, bringing your own hot tub is not allowed.

Other frequently asked questions
Customer service
What does the notation +pvm/mpm next to the calls mean?
Our customer service number 0300 870 900 is a paid service, costing €0.60/min, with a maximum charge of €3 + local/mobile network charges. Please note that waiting times are also charged.

For owners, the number 0300 870 922 is free of charge, excluding local/mobile network charges.

Note: "PVM" refers to the local network charge, and "MPM" refers to the mobile network charge. The cost is determined by your phone operator and your calling plan. Please check with your provider for the specific rates.

How quickly will my call request be answered?
If you have left a call request to our customer service, we will call you back as soon as possible, either the same day or at the latest, the next business day.

If your matter requires a response within the same day, we recommend waiting on the phone for a customer service representative to become available.

I don't want to be in contact by phone, and my matter is not urgent, what should I do?
If your matter is not urgent, you can contact us using the customer service form.

For urgent matters, please contact us by phone.
