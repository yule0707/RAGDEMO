Water Park and Day Spa
At the water park you can truly feel the warm Caribbean atmosphere! The spa features three water slides, several jacuzzis, an outdoor pool, a sauna village, a children's pool, and much more. If hunger or thirst strikes, you can conveniently grab a meal at Hesburger located inside the water park. You can also enjoy relaxing pampering moments with Harmony Spa's soothing treatments.

Stay at Turun Caribia
At Turun Caribia, you can stay the way you prefer, either in a hotel room or a holiday apartment. A buffet breakfast is always included in the price of a hotel room. In our well-equipped holiday apartments, you can enjoy your own peace and a private kitchen.

Parking

Parking in the area costs 1.60 €/h or 15 €/day. There are charging stations for electric cars in the parking area in front of the Villas 1 building. Parking is paid during charging.
